History

Pacesetters Foundation was established in 2015 to respond to gaps in education and health in underserved communities...

Our Aims

  • Increase access to inclusive quality education
  • Improve maternal and child health outcomes
  • Support youth employability

Objectives

  1. Reach 5,000 students with educational support by 2026
  2. Deliver 100 health outreaches annually
  3. Train 1,500 youth in vocational skills

Mission

To empower underserved communities to reach their potential through integrated education, health and livelihoods interventions.
