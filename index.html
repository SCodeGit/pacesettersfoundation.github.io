Who We Are

Founded in 2015, Pacesetters Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to transforming lives through education, community health, and social inclusion.

Our History

We began as a small initiative supporting rural schools in the Eastern Region. Over the years, our outreach has expanded to more than 120 communities nationwide, improving literacy, healthcare access, and livelihood opportunities.

Our Mission

To empower underserved communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable development programs that create long-term impact.

Aims
  • Enhance educational access for children and youth.
  • Promote health awareness and well-being.
  • Foster community participation and leadership.
Objectives
  • Train teachers and youth leaders.
  • Distribute educational materials annually.
  • Host national community outreach events.
Vision

A Ghana where every community thrives through opportunity, education, and empowerment.

Our Core Programs

Education & Literacy

Providing school resources, scholarships, and mentorship for students in underserved areas.

Health & Well-being

Delivering medical screenings, hygiene campaigns, and maternal health programs.

Skills & Empowerment

Training youth in digital skills, entrepreneurship, and sustainable livelihoods.
